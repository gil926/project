nizan nursery

Nitzan Nursery in Kiryat Ono is a spacious nursery that is immersed in green and offers a huge selection of potted flowers and plants. In addition, the nursery has a spectacular flower shop, with professional flower weavers who will give you a personal and warm treatment, and will help you weave bouquets and flower arrangements, dreams for any purpose and event. The store delivers flowers and gifts throughout the country. A writing field where customers can register and receive initial notifications about discounts, promotions, etc.
